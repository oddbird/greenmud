Into The Green Green Mud

A Novel (with pictures)

by Eric A. Meyer

This is only a demo of what's coming. Read the sample pages »
There's so much more fun to be had. Become a part of project »

A novel of association, reference, sounds and images.

Words move, images shift and characters wander through the pages. The clouds come and go. And so on. Entropy is the passage of more than time. Sometimes people. Mostly ketchup. Love is only what it is, and often only a shadow of that. The sun moves from the three hand to the four and squirrels are collecting nuts, so where does that leave us? Alone and alone and alone.
