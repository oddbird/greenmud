Eric A. Meyer is a multimedia artist, specializing in collaborative creation. Formerly Artistic Director of New World Arts and Technical Director of The LIDA Project, Eric co-founded OddBird, building custom web sites and applications; Teacup Gorilla, a multimedia arts collective and rock band; and Vicious Trap, a writing & performance collaboration.

OddBird is Carl Meyer, Eric Meyer, and Jonny Gerig Meyer. They built Susy for web designers, and they are core contributors to Compass, Django, pip, virtualenv and other open-source projects. They recently built MozTrap with Mozilla, and they are working on Portfoliyo tools for teachers, parents, and students.

Teacup Gorilla is Daniel Eisenstat, Sondra Eby, and Eric Meyer, a petite & deadly rock band and arts collective. They have been working and fighting together for over 10 years on projects ranging from math-punk to folk-rock, apocalyptic blues-rock, experimental theatre, and graphic design.
