Anatomy of a demonstration

I posted the first three chapters to give you a sense of the characters, format, and flow of the novel. However, only a few pages are fully designed and animated. The remaining pages have simple text, along with a still image of the first draft. I've added more page breaks, so some draft images will appear on multiple pages.

Tap/Click the edge of the page
or use the arrow keys
to turn pages.

Tap/Click the top of the page
or the gear icon
to show and hide the on-screen controls.

Browser support

Different browsers support different technologies. For the full experience try Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. Also Mobile Safari (iOS) and Mobile Firefox (Android) on tablets and phones. If you are using Internet Explorer, please upgrade or install the Google Chrome Frame plugin.
