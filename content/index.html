Into The Green Green Mud

A Novel (with pictures) by Eric A. Meyer

A novel of association, reference, sounds and images.

I wrote a novel with words and pictures. Now I'm adding movement and music, and working with Teacup Gorilla and OddBird to publish it online. We're excited to get started, but we need some money to cover our initial costs, and we need help spreading the word. In return we're offering a number of fun rewards, including the opportunity to become part of the process.

The team

Eric A. Meyer is a multimedia artist, specializing in collaborative creation, artistic process, experimentation, and performance. He is a founding partner of OddBird, building custom web tools, and Teacup Gorilla, a rock band and art collective.

Browser support

Different browsers support different technologies. For the full experience try Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. Opera and Android browsers work well, but are missing advanced features. If you are using Internet Explorer, please install the Google Chrome Frame plugin.
