He needs a woman.
Sometimes I wonder.
Something.
Could be the weather.
A man then?
He has a good job.
All that mud and gray sky.
In the park and dreaming.
Crying, I heard.
In the shadows.
In the park.
The same park.
The same bench.
He feeds the squirrels.
The same squirrels.
What's he dreaming?
