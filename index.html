Into The Green Green Mud

A Novel (with pictures) by Eric A. Meyer

What is this love thing, and where are you going?

Why are all the love stories obsessed with eternity and fidelity? What happens when the clouds shift, people change, and your best friend moves to South Nebraska or worse? This is an associative novel—a mashup of words, phrases, images, movement & music—following JJ & Susy through love and into life. Teacup Gorilla are writing the music, and OddBird are writing the code.

The team
