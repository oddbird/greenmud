Into The Green Green Mud

A Novel (with pictures) by Eric A. Meyer

A novel of association, reference, sounds and images.

I wrote a novel with words and pictures. Now I'm adding movement and music, and working with Teacup Gorilla and OddBird to publish it online. We're excited to get started, but we need some money to cover our initial costs, and we need help spreading the word. In return we're offering a number of fun rewards for anyone who contributes.

The team
